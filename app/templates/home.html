Experience an Enchanting Escape in Kollam at

Munroe Island

Nestled in the heart of Kerala’s backwaters, EcoPulse Resort offers a tranquil retreat on the picturesque Munroe Island. Surrounded by lush greenery and winding canals, the resort blends eco-friendly luxury with breathtaking natural beauty. Guests can explore canoe rides, kayaking, and village walks, immersing themselves in the island’s rich culture and traditions.
